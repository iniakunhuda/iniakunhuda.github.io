Lembaga Sertifikasi Profesi (LSP) SMKN 1 Surabaya System



Year 2016
Built with Laravel, Bootstrap, jQuery

LSP or Lembaga Sertifikasi Profesi SMKN 1 Surabaya is a Professional certification institutions are BNSP supporting institutions responsible for implementing professional competency certification. LSP SMK Negeri 1 Surabaya, which was formed in a legal body and formed by the Education and Training Institute registered by BNSP. This website is useful for serving students or assessments to register online and find out what schemes can be taken. Work as frontend developer for this system to help building UI/UX and responsive website.
